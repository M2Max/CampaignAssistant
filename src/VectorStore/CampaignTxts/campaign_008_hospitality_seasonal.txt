Campaign ID: CAMP_008_HOSPITALITY_SEASONAL
Campaign Name: "Escape the Ordinary" Resort Holiday Season Campaign
Client: Serenity Bay Luxury Resort
Industry: Hospitality / Travel / Resort
Client Background: Serenity Bay is a 250-room all-inclusive luxury resort in Costa Rica. They needed to drive bookings during the competitive November-December holiday season and maximize revenue per available room (RevPAR).

Campaign Overview:
A multi-channel seasonal campaign targeting affluent travelers planning holiday vacations. Combined luxury positioning, family appeal, and romance elements to attract different traveler segments during peak booking season.

Marketing Approach:
- Luxury travel agency partnerships: 125 travel agencies with commission incentives
- Email marketing: 500K affluent travel audience across luxury travel databases
- Social media: Instagram/Facebook luxury travel positioning and user-generated content
- Paid search: High-intent keywords (luxury Costa Rica resort, holiday vacation, family getaway)
- Influencer partnerships: 8 luxury travel influencers and 4 family travel vloggers
- OTA listings: Optimization on Expedia, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, Airbnb Luxury
- Retargeting: Display ads to past site visitors and booking abandoners
- Public relations: Travel publications (Condé Nast Traveler, Travel + Leisure, etc.)
- Packaging: Create 5 themed packages (Couples Escape, Family Adventure, Girlfriend Getaway, etc.)

Budget Details:
- Total Campaign Budget: $245,000 USD
- Paid Search (Google, Bing): $85,000
- Social Media Ads: $50,000
- Email Marketing: $18,000
- Influencer Partnerships: $40,000
- Travel Agency Commissions: $25,000
- OTA Management & Optimization: $12,000
- PR & Press Relations: $10,000
- Creative Assets: $5,000

Campaign Duration: 90 days (September 15 - December 15, 2024)

Booking Source Breakdown:
- Paid Search: 25% of bookings (5,847)
- OTA & Agencies: 45% of bookings (10,635)
- Social Media: 12% of bookings (2,758)
- Email: 14% of bookings (3,247)
- Influencer: 4% of bookings (847)

Key Learnings:
- Travel agencies still drive 45% of luxury resort bookings despite digital focus
- Peak booking window: 5-7 weeks before travel (aligned with Thanksgiving/Christmas planning)
- Couples packages had highest margins but family packages had better volume
- User-generated content (guest photos) in Instagram ads had 2.1x higher CTR than professional photos
- Luxury travel keywords averaged $12.50 CPC but had 4.7% conversion rate (profitable)
- Mobile bookings accounted for 34% of conversions; desktop 66%

Campaign Outcome: Exceeded revenue targets by 31% ($11.6M vs. $8.8M target). Achieved 89% occupancy during peak season. Serenity Bay expanded budget for 2025 peak season and launched loyalty program to retain customers. Case study for luxury hospitality marketing effectiveness.

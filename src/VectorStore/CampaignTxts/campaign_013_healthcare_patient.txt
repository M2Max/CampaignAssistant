Campaign ID: CAMP_013_HEALTHCARE_PATIENT
Campaign Name: "Your Health, Your Way" Patient Acquisition Campaign
Client: MediCare Urgent Care Centers (8-location chain)
Industry: Healthcare / Medical Services
Client Background: MediCare operates 8 urgent care centers in mid-sized cities. They needed to increase patient volume, build brand awareness among younger demographics, and position urgent care as alternative to expensive ER visits.

Campaign Overview:
A multi-channel patient acquisition campaign highlighting convenience, affordability, and quality care. Targeted health-conscious millennials and Gen-Z, parents, and employed adults seeking alternatives to traditional ER care.

Marketing Approach:
- Social media: Instagram, TikTok featuring quick health tips, patient testimonials, wait time updates
- Paid search: Google Ads targeting urgent care, walk-in clinic, flu shot, minor injury keywords
- Local SEO: Google My Business optimization for each location, local directory listings
- Influencer partnerships: 12 local health/wellness micro-influencers
- Patient testimonials: Video testimonials from real patients (diverse backgrounds)
- Educational content: Blog posts on common urgent care conditions and when to visit
- Email marketing: Newsletter with health tips, seasonal reminders (flu season), location-based offers
- Community partnerships: School nurse partnerships, workplace wellness programs
- App & online tools: Online check-in, appointment booking, health records access
- Promotional campaigns: Monthly promotions (free blood pressure check, flu shots, etc.)

Budget Details:
- Total Campaign Budget: $267,000 USD
- Paid Search (Google Local Services Ads): $95,000

Campaign Duration: 365 days (annual program)

Key Learnings:
- Local SEO and Google My Business critical for medical service location-based business
- App development paid for itself in first 6 months through reduced no-shows and convenience
- Patient testimonials (video) were most trusted content type, driving 3x higher conversion than medical facts
- Mobile optimization essential: 67% of traffic from mobile, 4.2% mobile conversion vs. 2.1% desktop
- Influencer marketing most cost-effective per patient ($9.82 vs. $3.47 for search, but highly targeted)
- Paid search scale limited by keyword volume; local SEO provided sustainable long-term traffic
- Promotional campaigns (free blood pressure screening) drove foot traffic but not high-value visits
- Virtual consultations became 8% of revenue stream post-launch, high-margin business

Unexpected Discoveries:
- 23% of patients came due to Google map reviews (review rating was #2 discovery driver after ads)
- Parents trusted influencers for children's health information more than they trusted medical professionals
- Weekend appointment availability became key differentiator in search ads
- TikTok unexpectedly effective with Gen-Z (24% of new patients, surprising finding)

Campaign Outcome: Exceeded patient volume target by 38% (52,974 visits vs. 38,500 target). Improved brand awareness from 18% to 67% in target markets. MediCare opened 3 additional locations based on demand. Patient retention improved, creating stable recurring revenue base. Successfully positioned urgent care as convenient, affordable alternative to ER. Campaign became template for expansion into new markets.

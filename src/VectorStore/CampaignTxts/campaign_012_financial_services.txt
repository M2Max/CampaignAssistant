Campaign ID: CAMP_012_FINANCIAL_SERVICES
Campaign Name: "Smart Wealth Builder" Financial Services Campaign
Client: ProWealth Advisors
Industry: Financial Services / Wealth Management
Client Background: ProWealth Advisors is an independent wealth management firm targeting high-net-worth individuals and small business owners. They needed to attract AUM (Assets Under Management) and establish thought leadership in personal finance.

Campaign Overview:
A comprehensive thought leadership and lead generation campaign positioning ProWealth as trustworthy financial advisors. Combined educational content, webinars, and strategic media placement to attract affluent prospects.

Marketing Approach:
- Thought leadership content: Weekly blog posts on investing, tax strategy, retirement planning
- Video content: Educational series on YouTube (wealth building, tax optimization, investment strategies)
- Podcast: "Wealth Conversations" featuring interviews with successful business owners and entrepreneurs
- Webinar series: Quarterly webinars on hot financial topics (tax planning, market volatility, retirement)
- Media placement: Op-eds in financial publications and local business media
- Paid search: High-intent keywords around wealth management and financial advice
- LinkedIn advertising: Targeting high-income earners and business owners
- Event marketing: Sponsorships of local business events and networking organizations
- Email marketing: Segmented newsletters by investor type (accredited, business owners, retirees)
- Strategic partnerships: Referral relationships with CPAs, attorneys, business advisors

Key Learnings:
- LinkedIn proved most cost-effective for affluent B2B audience ($18.74 cost per lead vs. $26.37 for search)
- Webinar format drove highest-quality leads with 74% attendance and 34% immediate consultation requests
- Strategic partnerships (CPAs, attorneys) generated 24% of leads at lowest acquisition cost
- Educational content (webinars, videos, podcasts) positioned firm as thought leader vs. salesy competitor
- Email nurture to existing lead list was highest-ROI channel (recurring cost minimal)
- Paid search keywords with "fee-only" or "fiduciary" had 2.3x higher conversion rates
- High-income demographics required longer sales cycle (73 days average) but higher close rates

Unexpected Discoveries:
- 12% of leads came from podcast guest referrals (cross-pollination effect)
- YouTube and podcast built significant brand awareness even among non-converters
- 89% of acquired clients consumed educational content before sales contact (pre-educated prospects)
- Operating under "best interest/fiduciary" positioning generated 3x more leads than competitors
